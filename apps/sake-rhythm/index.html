SakeRhythm (サケリズム)

Healthy drinking support app for group gatherings

📱 App Store 公開予定

Features

  • 💧 Smart hydration reminders during drinking sessions
  • 🌅 "For Tomorrow" tips to prevent hangovers
  • 👥 Group management for 4-20 participants
  • 🍻 Japanese drinking culture optimized (一次会 to 一日飲み)
  • 🏝️ Dynamic Island integration (iPhone 14 Pro+)
  • 🚨 Emergency consultation screen (non-medical)
  • ⏰ Phase-based health advice (Early/Middle/Late/Final)
  • 📱 iOS Native with SwiftUI & SwiftData

Perfect For

  • 🎉 Group drinking organizers (幹事) who want to enjoy too
  • 💧 People who want better hydration during drinking
  • 🌅 Anyone who wants to avoid hangovers
  • 👥 Groups of 4-20 people having drinking sessions
  • 🇯🇵 Those familiar with Japanese drinking culture
  • 📱 Users who appreciate beautiful iOS native apps

Drinking Session Types

  • 🥂 一次会のみ (Primary only) - ~2 hours
  • 🍻 二次会も (Secondary too) - ~3.5 hours
  • 🍺 ガッツリ飲み (Heavy drinking) - ~6 hours
  • 🍷 一日飲み (All-day drinking) - ~8 hours

App Info

Status: In Development (Construction Phase)

Target Release: Japan Market Only

iOS Compatibility: iOS 17.0+, iPhone (Dynamic Island: iPhone 14 Pro+ recommended)

Technology: SwiftUI, SwiftData, ActivityKit, Live Activities

Developer: Keisuke Karijuku

Important Safety Notice

⚠️ This app does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment advice.

In case of health concerns during drinking sessions, users should contact appropriate medical services or emergency services (119 in Japan) based on their own judgment. The app prioritizes human judgment over automated assessments.
